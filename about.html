About Us

Sea Supply Service Pte., Ltd

SeaSupply Catering is a premier provider of gourmet catering services, committed to delighting taste buds and exceeding expectations with every bite. With a team of culinary artisans and hospitality experts, we bring a wealth of experience and a flair for innovation to the table.

Sea supply catering services are tailored to meet the unique needs of maritime clients, ensuring quality meals and hospitality aboard ships and offshore installations. With meticulous menu planning and customization, they provide nutritious and varied options that accommodate diverse dietary preferences and requirements.

In addition to standard meal provision, they offer catering for special events, bar and beverage services, and offshore catering solutions to support workers in remote locations.

Compliance with food safety regulations, along with consultation and support for clients, are integral parts of their offerings.

Overall, sea supply catering services prioritize the well-being and satisfaction of crew members, passengers, and offshore workers while emphasizing sustainability and environmental responsibility.
