About Us

Sea supply catering services are tailored to meet the unique needs of maritime clients, ensuring quality meals and hospitality aboard ships and offshore installations. With meticulous menu planning and customization, they provide nutritious and varied options that accommodate diverse dietary preferences and requirements. These services oversee all aspects of galley and kitchen management, maintaining high standards of food safety, hygiene, and quality. In addition to standard meal provision, they offer catering for special events, bar and beverage services, and offshore catering solutions to support workers in remote locations. Compliance with food safety regulations, along with consultation and support for clients, are integral parts of their offerings. Overall, sea supply catering services prioritize the well-being and satisfaction of crew members, passengers, and offshore workers while emphasizing sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Our Services
Supply Chain Management

Specialized Services

Providing catering for special events such as weddings, corporate gatherings, or VIP functions held on ships.Catering for offshore installations and remote work sites, where access to food and supplies may be limited.

Galley and Kitchen Management

Managing onboard galleys and kitchens, ensuring they are properly equipped, clean, and compliant with safety standards.
